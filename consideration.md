# Considerations

**1. Merge Expand & Shorten into Paraphrase**
Combine both transformations under a single “Paraphrase” action with an option to expand or shorten, reducing key‑binding clutter.

**2. Remove Explain Action**
Treat explanation as a custom prompt or offer a generic “Transform” command—end users can use ChatGPT for ad‑hoc explanations.

**3. Niche Feature Suggestions**

- **Prompt Optimizer**: auto‑compress prompts for token efficiency
- **Outline/Bullet Extraction**: convert text into headings or bullet lists
- **Tone & Style Presets**: brand‑voice or persona transforms beyond simple tone
- **Speed‑Reading Mode**: highlight key sentences for rapid skimming
- **Snippet Manager**: history + clipboard manager for reusable boilerplate

**4. Monetization Model**

- **Subscription**: $5–7/mo (annual plan $50–70)
- **Lifetime License**: one‑time $49–69
- Free tier for grammar and basic paraphrase; Pro unlocks advanced workflows
